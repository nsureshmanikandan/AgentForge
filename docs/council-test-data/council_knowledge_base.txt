THE COUNCIL - DECISION INTELLIGENCE PLATFORM
Knowledge Base Document v1.0
============================================================

ABOUT THE COUNCIL
-----------------
The Council is a multi-agent decision intelligence platform where 5 specialized AI advisors
analyze complex business decisions from different perspectives, conduct blind peer reviews,
and a Chairman AI synthesizes a final verdict with confidence scoring.

ADVISOR PERSONAS
----------------
1. Contrarian Advisor
   Role: Challenges assumptions, identifies what could go wrong
   Focus: Risk identification, devil's advocate, surface hidden flaws

2. First Principles Advisor
   Role: Breaks problems down to fundamental truths
   Focus: Logical reasoning from basics, avoids inherited assumptions

3. Expansionist Advisor
   Role: Explores upside potential and growth opportunities
   Focus: Market expansion, blue-sky thinking, opportunity mapping

4. Outsider Advisor
   Role: Brings external perspective, industry benchmarks
   Focus: Competitive landscape, analogous industries, fresh eyes

5. Executor Advisor
   Role: Focuses on practical implementation
   Focus: Timelines, resources, dependencies, operational feasibility

DECISION PIPELINE
-----------------
Stage 1 - Intake: User submits decision with context, constraints, stakes
Stage 2 - Advisor Analysis: All 5 advisors analyze in parallel
Stage 3 - Peer Review: Blind cross-review between advisors
Stage 4 - Chairman Verdict: Synthesis with alignment matrix and confidence score
Stage 5 - Output: Recommendation, next steps, tradeoffs, key tensions

CONFIDENCE SCORING
------------------
90-100%: Strong consensus across all advisors, high alignment
70-89%: Majority agreement with minor dissent
50-69%: Moderate confidence, significant divergence
Below 50%: High uncertainty, recommend more information gathering

API ENDPOINTS
-------------
POST /api/decisions         - Submit new decision for analysis
GET  /api/decisions         - List all decisions with filters
GET  /api/decisions/{id}    - Fetch complete decision with verdict
POST /api/documents/upload  - Upload context file for RAG
GET  /api/health            - Backend health check

SAMPLE USE CASES
----------------
- Strategic pivot decisions (enter new market, change product direction)
- Technology investment decisions (build vs buy, platform selection)
- Hiring decisions (senior leadership, key technical roles)
- Partnership and M&A evaluation
- Risk mitigation strategy selection
- Budget allocation under uncertainty
